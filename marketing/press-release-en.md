# Press Release

## VendaCuraçao launches: private owners can now sell property in Curaçao without a traditional agent

**Willemstad, Curaçao — March 25, 2026** — VendaCuraçao has officially launched as a new online platform that enables private owners in Curaçao to sell their home, land plot, or apartment without relying on the traditional real estate agency model. The platform is built for sellers who want more control, direct buyer contact, and a more cost-efficient route to market while still maintaining a professional online presence.

VendaCuraçao positions itself as an FSBO platform — for sale by owner — tailored to the Curaçao real estate market. Sellers can submit their property, publish a trilingual listing, and receive leads directly. The concept addresses a clear gap in the local market, where traditional brokerage fees can often reach around 4% of the sale price.

“Many private sellers are willing to take a more active role in the sales process, but they lack the professional infrastructure to market their property properly,” said founder **Daniël Martina** of VendaCuraçao. “VendaCuraçao is designed to bridge that gap with less friction, lower cost, and more direct connection between owner and buyer.”

The company says the platform is relevant not only for owners living on the island, but also for Curaçao property owners based in the Netherlands or elsewhere who need a clearer, more modern way to manage a sale remotely. In addition to listings, the platform provides content and resources on pricing, leasehold structures, notary processes, neighborhood positioning, and seller preparation.

“The Curaçao market is ready for a modern direct-to-owner sales model,” Martina added. “Not every property seller wants or needs a traditional agent. In many cases, strong presentation, clear information, and fast follow-up create more value than a heavy intermediary structure.”

Listings are supported in Dutch, English, and Papiamentu. Over the coming months, VendaCuraçao plans to expand its partner network, educational content, and neighborhood-specific resources for owners and buyers.

VendaCuraçao is now live at **https://vendacuracao.com**.
